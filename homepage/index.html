Ronghui Xu (徐榕荟)

I am a PhD student from Decision Intelligence Lab, East China Normal University, supervised by Prof. Bin Yang. I completed my master’s degree at Shandong University in 2023, where I was advised by Prof. Meng Chen. I served as a reviewer for ICLR 2025 and KDD 2025.

My research focuses on Spatiotemporal Data Analysis. Feel free to reach out if you’re interested in my work!
